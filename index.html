Puzzle-Solutions-Archive

This is a store for daily solutions to various online puzzles, such as Wordle and Numble. The solutions are encoded in base64.

The following JavaScript Bookmark Applet might be of use to decode the base64 encoded text, when viewing a daily solutions text file.

Simply drag the link to your browser's Bookmarks Toolbar. Then click on it when viewing a YYYYMMDD.txt file from the archive. Any base64 text found on the open page will be listed along with its corresponding plain-text in a popup alert box.
